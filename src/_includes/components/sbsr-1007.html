Discover Hua Hin Moon Thai Massage

Hua Hin Moon Thai Massage, founded by Moon in 2012, brings authentic Thai healing traditions to Sunderland with over 15 years of professional expertise. Originally from Laos and trained in Thailand's renowned Hua Hin region, Moon combines traditional massage wisdom with British qualifications and professional standards. Our established clinic delivers genuine therapeutic treatments in a spotlessly clean, welcoming environment, committed to providing personalised care that transforms lives through the healing power of authentic Thai massage.
